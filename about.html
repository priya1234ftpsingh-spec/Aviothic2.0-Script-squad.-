About KIT Lost & Found

This demo is built as a hackathon project for Kanpur Institute of Technology. The system helps students quickly report lost or found items, browse reported items, and connect with the finder/owner. Replace the KIT logo and campus photo in the assets folder for final branding.

How it works

  1. Student reports an item via the Report page.
  2. Item is stored locally (demo) and displayed on Lost/Found pages.
  3. Interested users can view details and contact the reporter.
